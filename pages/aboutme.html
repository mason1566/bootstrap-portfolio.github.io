Self-Learning

(I guess its also called autodidacticism.)

Over the years, I've taught myself to:

  • Play guitar
  • Design things I like in Photoshop
  • Screenprint t-shirts
  • Create and record music (In programs such as fl-studio & ableton)
  • Other things I can't remember right now...
Take a look:
When Did I Start?

I started playing in 2020 when my brother gave me his 100+ year old guitar.

Where Did I Begin?

I began with an app called yousician. It's fun, but it is not a good way to become skilled at guitar.

Where Should You Start?

I highly recommend watching Justin Guitar. He's a fantastic teacher. He also has some of the best guitar courses available on his website (And they're free).

When Did I Start?

I started using photoshop right when I got my first laptop at 11.

Where Did I Begin?

I began by looking up simple tutorials & making funny photoshops of me & my family. Using it for 10 years now, it's an application that I feel very confident in.

Where Should You Start?

Theres so many ways to start designing. You don't need to use photoshop and could just do it on paper/any medium you'd like. If you would like to use photoshop, I recommend starting just like I did. A good youtube resource for photoshop tips & tutorials is PiXimperfect.

First cover design I was really proud of.

When Did I Start?

My brother and I started printing shirts last year (2020).

Where Did I Begin?

I've always loved fashion and art, and I randomly asked my brother if he wanted to start a t-shirt company. We started making designs for t-shirts, and after buying a shirt-press, we started printing our designs.

Where Should You Start?

Screenprinting your own shirts can be a tricky process, but if you want to start, I'd recommend going to michaels and getting a few materials from there. Don't go big until you decide if it's worth your time & you actually enjoy it.

When Did I Start?

I started lightly messing around in FL-Studio in mid-2018. In 2020, I made the first beat/instrumental that I was actually proud of.

Where Did I Begin?

I began watching youtube tutorials for fl-studio and got to learn the DAW (Digital-Audio Workspace). I started with simple loops and began to work up from there. 2021 is the year that I actually started to like the stuff I was making. That year, a beat I made got over 10k views.

Where Should You Start?

Go ahead and grab a copy of fl-studio (or ableton) and watch a few tutorials. You need to get used to the workspace or else it's gonna be a big hinderence to your music making ability (& creativity)
